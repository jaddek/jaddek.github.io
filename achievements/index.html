May - September, 2024

As part of my self-education and continuous learning, I was collaborating with team of recruiters from Eastern Europe.

I helped automate hiring and onboarding by creating and integrating a new platform for a huge team, which made their talent acquisition more efficient.

I learned a lot about how large recruitment teams are organized and how they select top talent for major companies.

Solutions:

  • All data from spreadsheets was transferred to the internal team circuit.
  • Role-based access to the team's business information was provided.
  • Teamwork on vacancies for working with candidates was implemented.
  • A mechanism for sharing candidates between recruiters was implemented.

Achievements:

  • The team's scalability was increased.
  • The risk of sensitive data leakage was reduced.
  • System logging made it possible to resolve conflict situations both within the team and resolve issues related to client requests.

The team’s agile approach to development allowed me to explore and implement cutting-edge features, making our solutions modern.

New features from Symfony 7.1 were implemented on the project:

  • Mapping Boolean Query String Parameters - Working with null values ​​from query string out of the box. Amazing!
  • Mapped Route Parameters - Smart entity injection allowed us to reduce our code base by almost 200 lines of code. Small things, but the code readability increases.
  • Improved twig:lint Command -Finally, convenient validation of data templates on twig, now the risk of errors in templates has become even lower!
  • New #[Assert\Type('list')] List validation - very useful.
  • Emoji Improvements - The Symphony team’s focus on optimizing dependency size was evident when they made emoji handling a separate dependency. This approach is improvement for this project, as it minimises unnecessary overhead, reduces build size, and saves resources and a make the costs a bit lower.
